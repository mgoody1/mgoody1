The channel

This channel primarily started as a gaming channel on youtube. That is where my passion was, but trying to make it as another gaming channel on YouTube isn't the easiest thing to do. Once I got my first gaming PC, I decided to try other things. That is where, I guess you could say, my channel focus on reviewing my PC for various games, or options that people were curious about. Since this PC is more on the "budget" end for a gaming PC, plenty of people were thinking about getting it, they just wanted to make sure it was right for them. That's when the channel started to take off.

About Me

You see I, like many others, always dreamed about making it as one of those famous content creators on the platform. Something about that lifestyle was always so appealing to me. It wasn't even the money that appealed to me most, more the popularity, and basically being my own boss. Being able to lead my own future to where I wanted to go has always been a dream of mine. That and I've always used YouTube videos as my "escape" on days or times when I just needed that break from reality. There were always the same creators that I would watch to achieve this goal, and if I could be that escape for even one person, what an awesome feeling that would be. However like I stated, growing as a gaming channel on YouTube is a struggle all too real. Once that transitioned over though, my subscriber count start growing and a rapid pace for my channel. In January of 2021 I had a count of 17 subscribers on my channel. By the end of March that number had grown to over 400, and averaging over 20 watch hours per day. Wow that feeling of growth, and all the positive feedback was an amazing feeling.

The Slowdown

So my channel was growing rapidly for my size, great thing right? Well behind the scenes I was trying to start something to change my life in more probable ways. You see, as big of a dream as that "YouTube" career was for me, even with seeing this bit of hope, it's still a longshot. That combined with my approaching age 30 in the coming months, I wanted to start something that had much better odds and changing my life. A line that Markiplier used during the ending livestream for Unus Annus, "You can dedicate your life to something for a year, and change your life forever", really stuck with me. So in comes Lambda. At Lambda School I could take a year to learn full stack web development, and change my life forever.
